Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HabiTick, pricing, and how it compares to apps like TickTick.

What makes HabiTick the best minimalist habit tracker app? ▼
HabiTick combines habit tracking and daily task management in one minimalist, guilt-free interface. Unlike traditional habit apps that penalise you for missed days, HabiTick includes Smart Streaks, Streak Shields, and 48-hour backdating so your real progress is always protected.
Why is HabiTick a top TickTick alternative? ▼
TickTick and other productivity tools require expensive monthly or yearly subscriptions. HabiTick offers a clean, focused habit tracker and task app for a single lifetime payment of £5.99 — pay once and use it forever without recurring fees. Read our full HabiTick vs TickTick comparison.
How do Streak Shields work? ▼
Streak Shields are earned automatically by building consistent habits and completing tasks. If life gets busy or you take an unexpected rest day, a Streak Shield protects your hard-earned streak from resetting to zero.
Can I use HabiTick on both Web and Android? ▼
Yes! HabiTick works in any web browser at app.habitick.app and is available as a native app on the Google Play Store for Android devices.
Is HabiTick available on iOS / iPhone? ▼
Yes! HabiTick runs smoothly on all iOS devices (iPhone & iPad) as a web app. Simply open app.habitick.app in Safari, tap the Share button, and select 'Add to Home Screen' for an instant app experience. We are also working on a native iOS App Store release!
Is HabiTick free to use? ▼
Yes! The Free plan gives you unlimited habit tracking, to-do task management, smart streak tracking, completion analytics, holiday mode, and daily mood journaling. Upgrading to Pro (£5.99 lifetime) unlocks 5 Streak Shields and personalized AI insights, powered by Google Gemini!
